About STC

The IEEE Software Technology Conference (STC) is a leading software conference that targets software practitioners from industry, government, and academia. STC provides a forum for authors, presenters, and participants to hear from prominent researchers and practitioners in the field as well as share opportunities, challenges, and successes.
