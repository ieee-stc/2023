NIST Registration

The Software Technology Conference (STC) will be held October 3 - 6, 2022 online.

Originally IEEE STC29 was planned to be held at NIST in 2020. Since NIST is a controlled-access facility, it required additional registration for non NIST employees to physically attend the conference. Since the new STC 2022 conference is going to be fully virtual, there is no need for a separate NIST registration anymore.
